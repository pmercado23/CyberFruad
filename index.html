Cyberfraud and its Main Targets

Cyberfraud is deliberate deception for unfair or unlawful gain that occurs online. Just in the last two years alone, cyberfraud has increased 25% from $1743.22 per attacked capita in 2012 to $2874.64 per attacked capita in 2014. Although one might suspect population-dense states to have more cyberfraud, we have observed that states such as Nebraska and Connecticut suffer the most from cyberfraud with an astonishing $4302 and $5001 lost per attacked capita in 2014, respectively.
